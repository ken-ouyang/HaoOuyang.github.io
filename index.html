Hao Ouyang (欧阳豪)

I am now a Ph.D. student at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Dr. Qifeng Chen. Prior to this, I got my Bachelor's degree at CUHK in 2018, advised by Prof. Jiaya Jia . I got my MPhil degree from HKUST in 2020, advised by Dr. Qifeng Chen. I am now a research intern at MSRA. I was a research intern at SenseTime from 2016-2017, working with Dr. Qiong Yan . I was also a research intern in Tencet Youtu-X Lab in 2017, working with Prof. Jiaya Jia.
